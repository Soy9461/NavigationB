This is a demo, that shows how to integrate a JS-Graph Applicaton inline.

A Graph Application can use the complete client area of the browser or just a part of the browser client area. Here we have a sample that uses a DIV to group additional DIVs that contain a toolbar, the canvas for the graph inside a DIV and a color palette to assign a fill color to the selected objects.

Simply select a drawing tool to add new objects to the drawing area. You can also copy and paste objects and change their arrangement.
